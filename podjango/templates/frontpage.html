Free as in Freedom

Free as in Freedom is a bi-weekly oggcast, hosted and presented by Bradley M. Kuhn and Karen Sandler. The discussion includes legal, policy, and many other in the Free, Libre, and Open Source Software (FLOSS) world. Occasionally, guest join Bradley and Karen to discuss various topics regarding FLOSS.
